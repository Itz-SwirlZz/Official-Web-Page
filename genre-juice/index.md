# Genre Juice Records / Podcast

A media collective within the Marcus Wonyâ Sullivan ecosystem.

## [Genre Juice Records](https://www.crunchbase.com/organization/genre-juice-records)

Genre Juice Records is an independent label and creative collective built to support and organize genre-bending creative work.

It functions as a structural home for multiple artistic expressions that would otherwise exist separately.

Current core projects include:
- Peace Martini (ambient, guitar-driven experimental music)
- $IZZURP (introspective rap and lyrical experimentation)

The label exists to preserve creative variety while still keeping it connected under one system.

It operates as:
- a release space for music
- an archive for evolving artistic identities
- a framework for experimental output across genres

---

## [Genre Juice Podcast](https://youtube.com/@genrejuicepod)

Genre Juice Podcast is a long-form audio and ASMR-influenced content space.

It began as a music-focused idea but evolved into a more open conversational format that prioritizes dialogue and accessibility.

The podcast includes:

- conversational episodes with friends and guests

- discussions that may involve disagreement, including political topics (such as LGBT and trans rights discussions)

- ASMR-style content, including relaxed speech and readings

- storytelling and casual reflection-based episodes

- reading children’s books as a form of calming or sleep-oriented audio content

The purpose is atmosphere:
It is designed to function as background listening, sleep audio, and long-form reflective content.

It serves both personal and audience-facing roles:
- a creative outlet for unfiltered expression

- a space for dialogue and perspective exchange

- a calming or ambient listening experience for viewers
