## Overview (External Layer)

<i>"I don’t try to prove I'm the best rapper in the room. I try to be the right rapper for that moment."
-$IZZURP</i>

$IZZURP is the primary rap-focused branch of my creative work, centered around layered lyricism, emotional contrast, replay value, and experimentation through delivery.

The project exists within Genre Juice Records and functions as both a personal outlet and an evolving creative archive.

Some tracks are introspective and emotionally heavy. Others are playful, abstract, chaotic, sarcastic, or intentionally difficult to fully decode on first listen.

The goal is not immediate understanding.
The goal is discovery.

## Internal Meaning (Personal Layer)

$IZZURP exists as the most direct and emotionally exposed branch of my creative system.

What started as performance-driven experimentation gradually evolved into a layered creative identity built around interpretation, atmosphere, pacing, emotional honesty, and replayability.

A major part of the project revolves around delayed understanding. Some lyrics are intentionally dense, fragmented, or structured in ways that reveal additional meaning over time.

I treat delivery as part of the message itself, not just the vehicle carrying it.

The name itself originates from a small creator I once followed online who passed away and slowly disappeared from internet memory over time. I adopted and altered the name as a way of preserving that memory while building something personal from it.

Because of that, themes of digital memory, forgotten media, lost context, emotional residue, and reinterpretation naturally appear throughout the music.
